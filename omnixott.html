TV Remote Shortcuts – How to Use

1. Navigating to Bottom Bar

If you have difficulty selecting options in the Bottom Bar directly (Home Page, Free Page, Live Page, TV Pro Page, or Sports Page), press the Menu button on your TV remote.
This will instantly move the cursor to the Bottom Bar, allowing you to easily select the page using the arrow keys.

2. Opening Video Player Settings

While watching a video, press the Menu button on your remote. This will open the Settings Bar of the video player.

In the Settings Bar you can:

  • Change Video Quality
  • Manage Subtitles
  • Change Audio Track
  • Access other player settings
